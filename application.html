The application is composed of several features including the ability to book seats, make payment for the booked seats. Customers that make frequent payments for tickets through the app get discounts on future ticket purchases. The application is structured as shown in the images below;

Location Selection

Here, a user shall be able to choose a branch of Cinemax that they would wish to watch their preferred movie from. The locations are three and they include Metroplex Naalya, Acacia Mall Kisementi and Arena Mall.

Movies Showing Now.

Here, a customer shall be able to see all the movies that are available at the moment and be able to choose a movie of their liking to watch. The movies range from horror to action, Sci-Fi, comedies, animations and season finales and premiers of selected series.

The Details Page

Here, the customer is able to read more about a movie they could be interested in watching. The details shown include the rating of the movie by popular movie reviewers and the duration of the movie. After reading through the details, an interested customer is able to book a seat in the cinema inside the app.

The Time Selecton

The user is able to choose their preferred time and date to watch the movie depending of their preference.

Seat Selector

This particular page enables the user to be able to choose a seat of their choice. Once a seat is picked, it is nolonger available and the user is only allowed to choose form the available seats.

Preview of the purchased ticket

Here, the user is able to see a preview of their purchased ticket with their photo attached for clarification of their identity. This ticket is what is presented to the security personel when getting in to the cinema.

Payment for the ticket

Having seen a preview of the ticket, the user then proceeds to make payment for the seat(s) that have been choosen. This is made possible through the incorpration of the momo API that enables mobile money payments.

Profile Page

Here, a user is able to see all their purchased tickets over a given period of time. They are also able to see the points that they have accumated that are awarded after every ticket purcahse. The user is give a unique ID which is included in the QR code for their unique identity to the movie that they have decided to watch and the seat number.

Profile Page

Here, a user is able to see all the movies and tickets that they have paid for over time. This also helps a user identify themselves when they arrive at the cinema using a unique QR code that is shown on the tap of the "View QR code" button
