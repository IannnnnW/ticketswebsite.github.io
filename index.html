Problem Statement

Here in Uganda, our team identified a tonne of loop holes in the entertainment sector especially when it comes to cinema. One of the issues that stood out were the long queues movie lovers make when a movie has just been released on to the cinema circuit. This is also accompanied by hard-cash payment problems such as lack of change at the ticket booths.

Solution

Our solution to the above problem is a mobile based application that supports these features ie, booking and purchase of a ticket and opens a possibility for better interaction between the business and customers through reviews and suggestions
